CPT208 / HUMAN-CENTRIC COMPUTING / B4

Designing playful ways for generations to talk, remember, and create together.

Between Us is a mobile-first web prototype for grandparents, parents, and children. It uses conversation prompts, memory triggers, and shared time capsules to make family interaction feel lighter, warmer, and easier to start.

Problem

Family members often care about each other, but do not always know how to begin meaningful conversation.

Opportunity

Playful prompts and co-created digital keepsakes can reduce awkwardness and support emotional connection.

Prototype scope

Conversation cards, memory triggers, emotion responses, and a shared capsule preview.

Three playful features

Simple interactions designed to lower social pressure and invite storytelling.

The system does not try to replace family relationships. It gives them a softer entry point: a reason to ask, answer, laugh, and keep something together.

01

Question Shuffle

Random prompt cards make conversation feel less formal and more playful, especially for family members who do not know how to begin.

02

Memory Trigger

Photos, songs, food, and places give both generations concrete starting points instead of abstract emotional questions.

03

Shared Capsule

A tiny co-created record turns one conversation into something visible, savable, and revisitable across time.

Use scenario

A 10-minute family interaction, designed for everyday life.

01

A child opens the mobile site before dinner and chooses Grandparent × Child.

02

The system draws a prompt card, then suggests a memory trigger such as old photos or a childhood song.

03

Both users pick an emotion, answer the prompt together, and save a short capsule for future sessions.
