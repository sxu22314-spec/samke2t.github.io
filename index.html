Bridging Generations Through Play

We designed a playful interactive system to strengthen communication and emotional connection between grandparents, parents, and children.

Research

Our project focuses on intergenerational relationships in hybrid settings. We explored how playful activities can help family members connect in more meaningful ways.

  • Stakeholders: grandparents, parents, children
  • Device: mobile
  • Context: hybrid interaction

Prototype Ideas

We brainstormed features such as family challenge cards, shared memory collections, and collaborative storytelling tasks.
