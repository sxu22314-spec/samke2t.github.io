5. Playful Requirements

3 Must-Haves to bridge the gap

Must-Have 1

Async & Sync Mini-Games

Provides low-pressure games like "Draw&Guess" or "Memory Flips" with integrated voice messaging, allowing generations to interact across time zones.

Must-Have 2

Collaborative Memory Puzzles

Turns old family photos into interactive puzzles. Solving them together unlocks "Memory Stories," turning static heritage into a shared achievement.

Must-Have 3

The Digital Family Garden

A virtual ecosystem driven by real-time sensor data. Uses visual metaphors like a growing tree to symbolize family health and connection.
